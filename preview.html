RCMP's Use of AI Surveillance Tools Revealed

2025-04-04
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Through a series of access to information requests, we've uncovered evidence of widespread AI surveillance tool adoption by the RCMP without public disclosure or oversight.

Key findings include:

> Undisclosed contracts with facial recognition vendors

> Use of predictive policing algorithms in major cities

> Lack of privacy impact assessments for AI tools
